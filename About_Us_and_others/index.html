Privacy Policy

At Jimin Books, we take your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that it is only used for the purposes for which it was collected. We will never share or sell your information to third parties without your explicit consent.

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you place an order or sign up for our newsletter. This information is used to process your order, send you updates and promotions, and improve our service.
